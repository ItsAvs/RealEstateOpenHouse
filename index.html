Information We Collect

From Listing Agents and Homeowners

  • Property details such as address, price, and listing description.

From Visitors at Open Houses

  • Personal details voluntarily entered in kiosk forms (e.g., name, email, phone number).

How We Use Information

  • To allow listing agents to manage and display property information.
  • To enable visitors to sign in at open houses and provide feedback via kiosk forms.
  • To help agents follow up with potential buyers.
  • To improve the functionality and reliability of the App.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may be shared in the following ways:

  • With the listing agent or their brokerage, for follow-up with interested buyers.

Data Retention
